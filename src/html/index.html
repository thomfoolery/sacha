About Me

Hi, I'm Sacha

My friends would tell you that I’m crazy creative, hyper-social and always starting collaborative projects.

I have ten years experience in the fashion industry doing everything from design, graphics, e-comm, marketing, branding, production, development, merchandising and buying.

I spend a lot of time making ceramics at a local clay studio. I’m a lousy cook. I could easily draw cartoons all day. I’m paranoid that my name confuses people. I really like to travel but don’t like the word wanderlust.

Currently residing in Seattle, WA.
